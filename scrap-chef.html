How to Play

The Loop: Mine scrap → build farms → grow crops → cook → serve at buffet → earn ⚙ Gears → upgrade tools → mine bigger scrap

Scrap Tiers:
• Flecks — tiny tan bits, 3 hits with rusty pick. Mine these first.
• Light scrap — 6-9 hits. Feasible early game.
• Heavy scrap — 100+ hits. Needs Power Drill or better.
• Core scrap — unbreakable. Place Auto-Miners (15 gears) for passive income.

Farming Pipeline:
Farm (click to plant) → Grower Bot (auto-replants, 3-tile radius) → Harvester Bot (picks ripe crops) → Conveyor Belts → Grill (cooks for bonus) → Combiner (merges ingredients into recipes) → Buffet (serves food)

Controls:
WASD/Arrows — scroll camera
Click — mine scrap, place buildings, plant crops
R — rotate belt direction (before or after placing)
Right-click — demolish building (50% refund)
ESC — deselect
Hold click — continuous mine with Power Drill or Plasma Cutter
U/🔧 button — upgrade shop

Farm & Feed

Getting Started:
1. Mine nearby flecks (small tan bits) to get scrap
2. Place a Farm, click it to plant
3. Place a Buffet where you want to serve
4. Build Belts from farm to buffet to deliver crops
5. Cook on a Grill for bonus gears
